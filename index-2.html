about us

Aniket Deshpande is a Trauma Informed Counseling Psychologist with over 3 years of experience of workingwith diverse populations, from children to middle aged, and facilitating them to address their needs andconcerns. He is dedicated to creating a safe and supportive environment for individuals to explore and heal fromtheir unpleasant and disturbing experiences. His approach integrates client-centered, trauma informed,somatic, attachment based, mindfulness, cognitive and compassion; with an understanding of each person’sunique journey and abilities.
