Privacy Policy

  • Privacy of Information: Your therapist cannot share what you discuss in therapy without your written permission.
  • Access to Records: You may request a copy of your therapy records.
  • Informed Consent: You will receive a confidentiality agreement before therapy begins.
  • Secure Storage: All records are securely stored to prevent unauthorized access.

  • Harm to Self or Others: If a client expresses serious intent to harm themselves or another, the therapist must take action.
  • Legal Orders: Therapists may be required to release records if ordered by a court.

Payment of Session Fees:

  • Clients must make timely payments via online payment methods.
  • Payment must be made before each session.
  • Failure to pay may result in session suspension.

  • Clients must be on time for scheduled appointments.
  • Notify the therapist at least 2 hours in advance if you anticipate being late.
  • Rescheduling requests should be made 24 hours in advance to avoid charges.
  • Missed appointments and last-minute cancellations will be charged the full session fee.
  • If the therapist cancels within 24 hours, the next session will be free.

Therapy is not a destination for us to reach, but it is a process where we explore, understand and work on various aspects which create distress. Therapy is a two way relationship, both you as my client and I will be responsible. As a client in therapy, you have certain rights that are important for you to know; and as a therapist, I have corresponding responsibilities to you.
My responsibilities as a therapist-
1. Whatever we talk and you share with me, all that information will be kept confidential.
2. If there are signs of self harm or harm to others, then with your permission and by letting you know, the confidentiality will be broken to a person with whom you feel safe and comfortable.
3. I'll make sure that the 1 hour of the session feels safe and comforting to you to share, vent out, be vulnerable, and take the steps ahead to achieve healthy well-being.
Your responsibilities as a client-
1. Turning up for the session at the decided time slot and day/ date.
2. Paying for every session in advance.
3. If due to any reason you want to reschedule the appointment, you have to inform me 24 hours before the session.
