About us

Trusted care, lasting positive change

The platform ‘Balance and Bliss’ is established to create a space which facilitates care and equipping individuals to attain emotional balance, mental clarity, and long-term psychological well-being. I envisage a society in which emotional and psychological care enables people to embrace their real selves, build resilience, and find inner peace. Facilitating the client with embodied presence and care, I aim to promote healing, growth, and connection by providing holistic, evidence-based care.

With a genuine interest in understanding the complexities of human behavior and a compassionate, client-centered philosophy, I aim to empower individuals to create positive change and live fulfilling lives. I firmly believe in the potential for growth and healing within each person and strive to create a safe, supportive space where they can explore, heal, and thrive.

My approach is centered on the belief that every person is unique and possesses the inherent ability to grow, heal, and transform. I am committed to helping clients move from unhealthy to healthy embodiment with self-compassion, facilitating them toward thriving in life

Firstly, I appreciate your move to seek therapy/ counselling for your mental and emotional wellbeing. Therapy is not a destination for us to reach, but it is a process where we explore, understand and work on various aspects which create distress. Therapy is a two way relationship, both you as my client and I will be responsible. As a client in therapy, you have certain rights that are important for you to know; and as a therapist, I have corresponding responsibilities to you.
My responsibilities as a therapist-
1. Whatever we talk and you share with me, all that information will be kept confidential.
2. If there are signs of self harm or harm to others, then with your permission and by letting you know, the confidentiality will be broken to a person with whom you feel safe and comfortable.
3. I'll make sure that the 1 hour of the session feels safe and comforting to you to share, vent out, be vulnerable, and take the steps ahead to achieve healthy well-being.
Your responsibilities as a client-
1. Turning up for the session at the decided time slot and day/ date.
2. Paying for every session in advance.
3. If due to any reason you want to reschedule the appointment, you have to inform me 24 hours before the session.

I have read this statement. I understand my rights and responsibilities as a client, and my therapist's
responsibilities to me. I agree to undertake therapy with Mr. Aniket Deshpande. I hereby declare that in case I take any drastic actions which may cause any harm to me / my life, Mr. Aniket Deshpande should not be held responsible. I understand that the details that I share here would be kept completely confidential.
